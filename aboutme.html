王鹏，崂山国家实验室副研究员。在中国海洋大学取得学士学位，美国迈阿密大学（University of Miami）获得博士学位，博士后师从美国加州大学洛杉矶分校（UCLA）McWilliams院士。曾任中山大学“百人计划”副教授，入选自然资源部高层次人才工程。主持和参与国家自然科学基金委项目、科技部重点研发计划等。主要研究方向为波浪—海流耦合及相互作用，在JPO, JGRO, JAMES, GRL等期刊上发表论文20余篇。

WANG Peng, an associate researcher at Laoshan Laboratory. He obtained his bachelor’s degree from Ocean University of China and his Ph.D. from the University of Miami, USA. His postdoctoral research was conducted under Academician James C. McWilliams at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). After returning to China, he served as an associate professor under the "Hundred Talents Program" at Sun Yat-sen University and was selected for the Ministry of Natural Resources' high-level talent project. He has been the principal investigator or a key participant in projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the Ministry of Science and Technology's Key Research and Development Programs. His primary research focus is on wave-current interaction, and he has published over 20 papers in journals such as Journal of Physical Oceanography (JPO), Journal of Geophysical Research Oceans (JGRO), Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems (JAMES), and Geophysical Research Letters (GRL).
