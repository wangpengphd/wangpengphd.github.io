  • Langmuir Circulation

    The free-surface boundary condition is applied such that surface waves are explicitly resolved. Langmuir number La = 0.4, wind stress 0.33N/m^2, wave amplitude 0.8m, wave length 45m, and wave period 6s. Domain size is 628m x 314m x100m. ( Youtube video )


    --------------------------------------------------

    Related papers:

  • Wang, P. , Özgökmen, T. M.. Langmuir circulation with explicit surface waves from moving-mesh modelling. Submitted to Geophysical Research Letters on May, 2017.

  • 3D Transport within Mesoscale Eddies

    Cylindrical domain with Radius = 2 and Height = 1. Initially, a small patch of tracer is released in the center of a vortex at mid-depth.
    1. Case rc216-5s: evolutions of passive tracer in a initially-stable vortex (Youtube video).


    2. Case rc216-5: evolutions of passive tracer in an initially-unstable vortex (Youtube video).


    --------------------------------------------------

    Related papers:

  • Wang, P., Özgökmen, T. M., 2015. How do hydrodynamic instabilities affect 3D transport in geophysical vortices? Ocean Modelling, DOI: 10.1016/j.ocemod.2015.01.002. (PDF)

    Cylindrical domain with Radius = 4 and Height = 1. Homogeneous fluid density. Case rc216-5:
    1. Vertical vorticity evolutions of an initially-unstable vortex; (Youtube video)


    2. Vertical velocity evolutions of an initially-unstable vortex, demonstrating (spiral) inertial waves; (Youtube video)
